What it is

What this group does.

Some decisions are easy to change and some are not. Strategy & Counsel is built for the second kind — the choices about where to play, how to fund, whether to buy or sell, what risk to carry, and how to cross a border. These are the moments where judgment, not headcount, decides the outcome.

The group spans six service lines. Strategy and value creation set direction and tie it to EBIT. Corporate finance and CFO services bring a senior finance seat. M&A and transactions handle the deals. Risk, resilience, and compliance keep you sound — including the new frontier of AI governance and the EU AI Act. Market entry lands you across the corridor, and sustainability builds what lasts rather than writing reports about it.

This is also the most natural bridge between Consulting and Capital. Corporate finance, M&A, and the structuring side of market entry flow straight into Capital Services when a decision becomes a deal — so the same team that advised the strategy can help execute the transaction.

Questions, answered

The things people ask first.

Why are corporate finance and M&A in consulting and not only in Capital?

Because the thinking starts here and the execution often moves to Capital Services. You get one team across the decision and the deal — the strategy, the structure, and the transaction — rather than a hand-off between firms.

Can I get a fractional CFO or CSO without a full engagement?

Yes. Fractional capability is one of our core formats — a senior strategy or finance seat, full weight and part time, for as long as you need it.

Is AI governance really a strategy and risk issue now?

Very much so in 2026. With the EU AI Act in force and India's framework evolving, governing the AI you deploy is a board-level risk and, increasingly, a billable advisory line — not an afterthought.

How does market entry connect to the rest of Aadi?

The go-to-market side sits here; the cross-border structuring side flows into Capital Services. Together they let you land in a new market cleanly — demand, entity, capital, and compliance.

Facing a decision that's hard to reverse?

Bring us the call — the strategy, the deal, or the risk. We'll give you judgment you can act on, and execution when it's deal time.
