What it is

The best of a network, coordinated by one firm.

No boutique should claim to do everything, and the firms that pretend to usually do most of it poorly. We take the opposite posture deliberately: a tight, senior core, partnered with best-in-class specialists for the rest, and coordinated so that you receive seamless, end-to-end value from a single relationship rather than a pile of vendors to manage.

The ecosystem spans the corridor and the disciplines that surround our work — specialist advisors, outsourced services, capital partners, technology, on-ground legal and tax, and data and diligence providers. We have vetted them, we have worked with them, and your Engagement Partner manages the seams so that nothing falls between the cracks.

Here are the categories of partner in the network.

The categories

Our partner ecosystem.

Vetted, coordinated, and corridor-wide — no logos, just capability.

Specialist advisors

Deep experts we bring in for niche needs — sector specialists, technical advisors, and strategy partners that augment our core.

Outsourced services

Compliance, accounting, back-office, and operational services — so your firm runs lean without building every function in-house.

Capital partners

Co-investors, lenders, and syndicate partners across the corridor — extending what our Capital arm can assemble for you.

Technology partners

Best-in-class tools and platforms we integrate and deploy, so that what we build for you stands on solid foundations.

On-ground corridor partners

Trusted legal, tax, and entity specialists across India, the US, and Singapore — the local footing that cross-border work demands.

Data & diligence partners

Market data, research, and diligence specialists that sharpen our advisory and investing work.

Questions, answered

The things people ask first.

Do you list your partners?

We describe the categories rather than parade logos — consistent with how we present the whole firm. The right partner is introduced when it fits your need.

Why partner instead of doing it all?

Because a lean, senior core plus best-in-class specialists beats a bloated firm pretending to expertise everywhere. You get depth without the overhead.

Who coordinates the partners?

We do. Your Engagement Partner manages the seams, so you get end-to-end value from one relationship rather than a roster to wrangle.

How do we become a partner?

Reach out via Contact with a little about your organisation and where you see a fit. If there's a real opportunity, we'll be in touch.
