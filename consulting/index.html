What it is

Advise, then build — in production.

Most consulting ends precisely where your risk begins: a strategy is handed over, and the hard part of making it real is left to you. Aadi's Consulting practice is built to cross that line. We do the analysis and we build the thing the analysis points to — the AI system, the operating model, the revenue engine, the deal — so the value is realised, not just recommended.

The practice spans fourteen service lines in four groups: Build (digital, data & AI, innovation, operations), People (organisation, transformation, family business), Growth (marketing/sales/customer, cost/pricing/revenue), and Strategy & Counsel (strategy, corporate finance, M&A, risk, market entry, sustainability). Most engagements start with the sharpest line and expand as we earn the right.

And because Consulting sits beside Capital and Cofoundry, the work rarely stays in one lane — a build surfaces a thesis, the thesis attracts capital, and the strongest become companies we co-found with you.
