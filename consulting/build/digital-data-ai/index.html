What it is

The problem, and how we solve it.

By 2026 almost every company has an AI ambition and a pile of pilots. Very few have AI actually running in production, owned by the business, earning money. The gap is rarely the model — it's the workflow design, the data underneath, the guardrails, and the operating discipline to run it. That gap is exactly what this service closes.

We design and build agentic systems that execute multi-step work inside your real processes — with the orchestration, the human-in-the-loop points, and the controls designed in from the start. Underneath, we build the clean, governed data layer that makes AI and analytics trustworthy rather than a liability. And we wrap it in governance that is audit-ready and aware of the EU AI Act and India's evolving framework.

This is operator work, not advisory. Senior engineers and designers build the thing; AI accelerates our own research and first drafts; and we stay until it's reliable in your hands. When a capability we build deserves to be its own company, it has a clean path into Cofoundry, our venture studio.

Questions, answered

The things people ask first.

Do you build the AI yourselves or just advise?

We build it. Senior engineers ship working, governed systems to production — we're operators, not a strategy unit handing you a vendor shortlist.

What exactly is 'agentic' AI here?

AI that executes multi-step tasks inside your real workflows, with designed guardrails and human checkpoints — not a chatbot bolted on the side.

Our data is a mess. Is that a blocker?

No — it's the norm, and usually the first thing we fix. The governed data layer is part of the work, not a prerequisite you must deliver first.

How do you handle AI risk and the EU AI Act?

Governance is built in: model-risk management, audit trails, and compliance designed for the EU AI Act and India's evolving rules — not bolted on after.

How fast can something be live?

An AI Readiness Sprint takes about 30 days; a first agent in production typically follows in weeks, not quarters — we build to ship, then expand.

Ready to put AI into production?

Tell us the workflow that's costing you time or money. We'll tell you, plainly, whether we can ship it and how fast.
