How it works

From advice to a company — without a handoff.

Conviction moves through the firm in one continuous motion. When something we back hits trouble, Errored Coin recovers the value and the lesson, and feeds it back to the start.

1

Advise

Consulting validates the problem and ships the working thing — no equity taken, no studio cash drained.

2

Build

Where there's a company, the studio co-builds the product and go-to-market with our own operators.

3

Fund

Capital backs the strongest at graduation — clean cap table, recycled cash, aligned incentives.

4

Co-own

We hold a founder-aligned stake and stay accountable — the upside, and the downside, owned together.

Questions

The things people ask first.

What does Aadi actually do?

We advise (Consulting), finance (Capital), and co-found companies (Cofoundry) across the India–US corridor — one firm, one accountable relationship, three ways to act.

Is Aadi one company or several?

One. Aadi Projects operates Consulting, Capital, and Cofoundry as trade names — deliberately simple, until a real trigger warrants more structure.

How do you handle being advisor and investor?

With a real firewall: advisory serves you, investing rests on our own thesis, confidences never cross, and conflicts are disclosed and reviewed.

Do you only work on the India–US corridor?

It's our focus and our edge. We occasionally work beyond it where the fit is exceptional, but the corridor is where we're deepest.

Tell us what you're building.

One note is usually enough to know whether we're the right room — and which part of the firm answers it.
