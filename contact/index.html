Good to know

A few quick answers.

Is this really the only way to reach you?

Yes — one inbox, on purpose. It goes to real people at the firm, not a ticketing queue. We don't use contact forms.

What should I include?

Who you are, what you're building or facing, and what you're hoping for — enough to point your note to the right part of the firm.

Will I get a real reply?

Yes. We read what comes in and respond ourselves. If we're not the right fit, we'll tell you straight.

Do you work outside the India–US corridor?

The corridor is our focus and edge. We occasionally work beyond it where the fit is strong.
